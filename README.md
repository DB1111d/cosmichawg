## Tools: Python3

### gitclone.py

This will clone whatever repo you've already cloned before to your downloads folder.

### start.py

Automates local host initiation and opens your index.html.

### addhshow.py

Creates a new entry to the shows.json

### addlineup.py

Creates a new entry to the lineups.json

### addhsetlist.py

Creates a new entry to the setlists.json

### gitpush.py

Commits new edits to your main repo.

### gitpush.py

Commits new edits to a branch of your repo.
