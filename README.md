Lineup Schema Rules 

Every show must have these 5 fields — id, venue, city, date, and lineup. The date must have a month that is one of the 12 valid abbreviations (Jan, Feb, etc.), a day between 1 and 31, and a 4-digit year. The lineup must have at least 1 member, and every member must have a name and a role that aren't blank. It doesn't care what the actual names or roles say, just that they exist.

Setlist Schema Rules

Every show must have these 5 fields — id, venue, city, date, and setlist. The date rules are identical to the lineup schema. The setlist must have at least 1 song, and every song must have a title that is one of the values in the song list.
